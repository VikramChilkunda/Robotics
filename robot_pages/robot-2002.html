    • FRC 589 - 2002: Zone Zeal

    Zone Zeal was the 2002 game for the FIRST Robotics Competition. In it, robots playing in alliances of 2 competed to move goals and balls into various zones within the playing field. Goals were worth more points if they had more balls in them and the points for the goals were awarded to either alliance depending on the zone which the goal was in at the end of the match. Bonus points at the end of the match were awarded to robots who were in their home zones.
